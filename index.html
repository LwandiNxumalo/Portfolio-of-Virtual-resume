About Me

My passion for technology started at a young age. I was always fascinated by cellphones, laptops, games, and applications like Mxit and Facebook, and I often wondered how they were built. Growing up in a disadvantaged community where careers in technology were rarely discussed, I didn't have many opportunities to learn about the IT industry. Instead of discouraging me, that lack of exposure fueled my curiosity and inspired me to pursue a career where I can build software that solves real-world problems and helps educate and empower young people in communities like mine.

I am currently studying for a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology at North West University, where I continue to strengthen my skills in software development, programming, databases, networking, and

web technologies. I enjoy programming because it challenges me to think creatively and solve problems. Every new concept I learn motivates me to keep improving, and my goal is to become a skilled Software Engineer or Web Developer, building innovative and impactful solutions that make a positive difference in people's lives.
